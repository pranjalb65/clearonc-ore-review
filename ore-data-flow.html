ORE Data and AI Flow

How data and the model move through ORE, left to right. Step through each stage in the side panel, or drag to explore. The cylinders are the Supabase stores. Every clinical decision and every flag is deterministic and cited (green). Three live model agents are used, only to read the record, ground wording in the literature, and write the explanation (amber). They never make or override a decision. All steps are shown. Only the amber ones use a language model, and none of them is a decision.

The same data and knowledge flow runs for every patient, every cancer type, and every source.
