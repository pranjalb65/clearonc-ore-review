Primary biomarker gate, full decision map

The drill-down behind one decision node. The same shape applies to any cancer (EGFR or ALK in lung, MSI or KRAS in colorectal); here the primary biomarker is HER2 by IHC. An equivocal or missing result does not pass through, it triggers a data request and the case holds until the result resolves.

How to read it: diamonds are checks, green are resolved results, purple are the pathway, blue is the record. Red is a data request, the engine asks for the missing test rather than assuming, and re-enters the gate when it arrives.
