ORE Master Decision Map

The cancer-agnostic decision skeleton for the full oncology continuum, from risk through end of life. Step through each stage in the side panel, or drag to explore. The skeleton is invariant across every cancer. The knowledge that fills each node (markers, staging system, regimens, criteria) is what varies. The Architecture (Breast) tab is the first worked drill-down, and the Source Registry tab pins the guideline behind each stage.

One skeleton, every cancer. Each stage is gated by the cross-cutting checks shown at the bottom of the map, and the content of each node is cancer-specific.
