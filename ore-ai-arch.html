ORE AI Architecture

How the standard parts of an AI system (knowledge graph, RAG, agents, skills, handoff, evals, fine-tuning, guardrails) compose into ORE. Step through each part in the side panel, or drag to explore. The center is a deterministic core: skills that look up cited rules in the knowledge graph. Everything a model does (amber) only reads, grounds, or explains. The decisions are deterministic and cited, the model never decides, and fine-tuning never touches the decision logic.

The same architecture runs for every patient and every cancer type. The knowledge graph is what varies, not the machinery.
