ORE Workflow Tree

The full decision space, left to right, guided. Use the side panel to step through each decision node, it pans, highlights, and shows the detail and citations. Or drag to explore freely. Every clinical decision and every flag is deterministic and cited (green). Three live model agents are used, only to read the record, ground wording in the literature, and write the explanation (amber). They never make or override a decision. All steps are shown. Only the amber ones use a language model, and none of them is a decision.

This tree is the ORE architecture; the same steps apply to any cancer type. Example: 62F, invasive ductal carcinoma, cT2N1M0 Stage IIB, ER+/PR+, HER2 IHC 3+, ECOG 1, on trastuzumab plus paclitaxel, DPYD untested, no baseline LVEF.
